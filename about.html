About UK Furry Community Hub

Connecting furry communities across the United Kingdom

Our Mission

The UK Furry Community Hub is dedicated to connecting and supporting furry communities across the entire United Kingdom. We believe that every furry deserves access to a welcoming, inclusive community where they can be themselves, make friends, and enjoy shared interests.

Our goal is to make it easy for furries to find local communities, join Telegram groups, attend meetups, and connect with fellow enthusiasts in their area. Whether you're in a large city or a small town, there's a place for you in the UK furry community.

Our Values

Inclusivity

We welcome furs of all backgrounds, experiences, and interests. Everyone deserves a safe space to be themselves.

Community

We believe in the power of community. Together, we're stronger and can create amazing experiences.

Safety

We prioritize the safety and well-being of all members. We're committed to maintaining a positive environment.

Accessibility

We work to make our communities accessible to everyone, regardless of location or circumstances.

What We Offer

Local Community Hub

Easy Search

Quickly find furry communities near you with our intuitive search and browse features.

Group Information

Learn about each community group, their focus areas, and how to join their Telegram communities.

Meetup Opportunities

Discover local fursuit meets, conventions, and social events happening in your area.

Community Guidelines

Respect & Consent

Always respect others' boundaries and consent. Photography and physical contact should always be consensual.

18+ Content

Keep adult content appropriate to the venue and audience. What's acceptable varies by group and event.

Inclusivity First

Discrimination based on identity, orientation, gender, race, or beliefs is not tolerated. Everyone is welcome.

Be Kind

Support each other, celebrate diversity, and create a positive environment for all community members.
